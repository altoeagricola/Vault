## Purpose
- Register events and promote mindfulness reflection


## Policies
- Daily folder is git ignored. 
	- They're expected to be raw, messy and intimate.
	- They're supposed to be reflected on, re-signified with healthy leans and extracted as soon as possible to feed other folders .
	- They're supposed be forgotten to avoid attachment and reduce noise.

- Notes that need to survive can be included in Week notes.
