American productivity educator and author of *Building a Second Brain* (2022). Creator of the [[PARA Method]] and one of the most prominent voices in the modern [[Personal Knowledge Management]] movement.

Forte's contribution was bridging classical productivity thinking ([[Getting Things Done]]) with the digital tools era. His "Second Brain" concept frames PKM not as mere note-taking but as building an external cognitive system — a digital extension of memory and thought. The PARA framework emerged from this work as a universal structure for organizing digital information by actionability rather than topic.

His course, *Building a Second Brain*, popularized PKM practices among knowledge workers and helped establish the field as a distinct discipline beyond traditional productivity advice.
