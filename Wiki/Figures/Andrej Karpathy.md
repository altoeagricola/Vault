Slovak-Canadian computer scientist and AI researcher. Co-founder of OpenAI, former Senior Director of AI at Tesla (leading the Autopilot vision team), and a widely recognized educator in deep learning through his Stanford lectures and YouTube content.

In the [[Personal Knowledge Management]] context, Karpathy is notable for publicly sharing his approach to maintaining an "LLM Wiki" — a personal knowledge base where a language model reads sources, extracts structured information, and maintains an interlinked wiki. This concept represents a frontier in PKM: shifting the human role from writer to curator and editor, using AI to dramatically increase ingestion throughput while preserving coherence and structure.
